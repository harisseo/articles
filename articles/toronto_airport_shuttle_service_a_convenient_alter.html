Shared vs. Private Shuttle Options

Airport shuttle services typically offer both shared and private shuttle options. Shared shuttles are a more economical choice, as you'll be sharing the vehicle with other passengers heading in a similar direction. You'll have the entire vehicle to yourself, making it a suitable option for families, business travelers, or those who value privacy and direct transportation.
