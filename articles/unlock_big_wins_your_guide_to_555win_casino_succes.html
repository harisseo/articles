Return to Player (RTP) and volatility are two important factors to consider when choosing which games to play.

For example, a game with an RTP of 97% is expected to pay back $97 for every $100 wagered. * Volatility: Volatility, also known as variance, refers to the level of risk associated with a game. Choosing games with higher RTP and lower volatility can increase your chances of winning small amounts consistently, while choosing games with lower RTP and higher volatility can offer the potential for larger payouts but also carry a greater risk of losing.
