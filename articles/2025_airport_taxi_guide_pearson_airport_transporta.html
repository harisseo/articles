Know Your Taxi Options at Pearson

Pearson offers two primary taxi services: Standard Taxis and Flat Rate Taxis. Standard taxis operate on a metered fare system, common for destinations within Toronto. For journeys to nearby cities like Mississauga or specific zones, licensed Flat Rate Taxis provide a predetermined, fixed price – a significant advantage for budgeting certainty against traffic delays. Official taxi ranks are located just outside the arrivals levels of both Terminal 1 and Terminal 3. Look for clearly marked signage directing you to the queue managed by the Greater Toronto Airports Authority (GTAA). Licensed taxis are uniformly identifiable, ensuring safety and regulated pricing. For those specifically heading to Mississauga, dedicated airport taxi mississauga services often offer specialized flat rates that can be more economical than standard fares.

Fare Structures and What to Expect

Understanding costs prevents surprises. Standard Metered Fares: Start with an initial charge (around $4.25 in 2025), then increment based on distance and time spent in traffic. Expect additional charges for airport fees ($5.00), tolls (like Hwy 407), and tips (15-20% is customary). Fares to downtown Toronto typically range from $55-$75. Flat Rate Fares: These are zone-based fares fixed by the GTAA for specific destinations like Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, or Markham. Always confirm the exact flat rate with the driver before departing. The rate includes the base fare and airport fee but excludes tolls and tip. Request a receipt.
