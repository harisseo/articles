What is AR/VR Poker?

AR/VR poker takes the classic card game and injects it with the power of augmented and virtual reality. Instead of staring at a flat screen, you're immersed in a 3D environment, interacting with other players as if they were right there with you. Augmented reality overlays digital elements onto your real-world view, while virtual reality transports you to a completely simulated world.
