Bankroll Management: Protecting Your Investment

Effective bankroll management is arguably the most critical element of a successful betting strategy. It involves setting aside a specific amount of money solely for betting purposes and managing it responsibly. A common rule of thumb is to never risk more than 1-5% of your bankroll on a single bet. This prevents significant losses from wiping out your entire investment. Proper bankroll management also includes tracking your wins and losses, adjusting your stake sizes based on your performance, and knowing when to stop – both when you're winning and when you're losing.
