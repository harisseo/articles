Understanding the House Edge

The foundation of any successful casino strategy lies in understanding the house edge. This is the inherent advantage the casino has over the player in any given game. It's expressed as a percentage and represents the average amount the casino expects to win from each bet placed over the long run. Games with a lower house edge offer better odds for the player. Roulette, depending on the variant (European vs. American), has a different house edge due to the presence of one or two zero slots.
