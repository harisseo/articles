Understanding the Basics: Blackjack Rules Refresher

Before diving into advanced strategies, it's crucial to have a firm grasp of the fundamental rules. Blackjack is played with one or more standard 52-card decks. The goal is to have a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's hand without exceeding 21 (busting). Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value, face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10, and an Ace can be worth either 1 or 11.

A hand consisting of an Ace and a ten-value card is called a "blackjack" and typically pays out at 3:2 odds. If both the player and the dealer have blackjack, it's a push (tie), and the player's bet is returned.
