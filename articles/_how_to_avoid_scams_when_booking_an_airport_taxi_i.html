"How to Avoid Scams When Booking an Airport Taxi in Toronto"

Always choose a licensed taxi or limousine service. Legitimate operators in Toronto must display a valid municipal license, which ensures they meet safety and insurance standards. Scammers often lure passengers with low initial quotes, only to inflate prices mid-ride. Before confirming your booking, ask for a fixed rate that includes tolls, taxes, and any additional fees. Verify Driver and Vehicle Details

Legitimate drivers will always share their name, photo, and vehicle details before pickup. Avoid Cash-Only Transactions

Many fraudulent drivers insist on cash payments to avoid leaving a paper trail. Opt for companies that accept credit cards or digital payments, as these methods provide a record of your transaction. Be wary of drivers who claim your pre-booked ride is unavailable and offer a “better deal.” Other tactics include taking unnecessarily long routes, demanding payment upfront, or refusing to start the meter.
