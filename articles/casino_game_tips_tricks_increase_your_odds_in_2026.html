Navigating the Roulette Wheel

Roulette, with its spinning wheel and seemingly random outcomes, might appear purely based on luck. However, understanding the different bet types and their associated odds is crucial. European roulette, featuring a single zero, offers a lower house edge compared to American roulette, which includes both a single and double zero.
